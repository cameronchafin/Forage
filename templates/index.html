Photo Identification

Forage's photo identification allows users to quickly and accurately identify various mushroom species from photos. This innovative feature uses advanced image recognition technology to analyze mushroom characteristics.

  • Upload photos of mushrooms and receive instant species identification.

Forage's Detailed Results Breakdown offers an in-depth analysis of each identified mushroom species. This feature aims to educate users about their findings, enriching their foraging experience.

  • Links to additional resources for further learning and exploration.
